Our Services

Plastic Collection & Sorting

At ECORECYCLE NEXUS, we employ dedicated collection points throughout the community where plastic waste is gathered, sorted by type, and prepared for recycling. Our team ensures that every piece collected is properly categorized to enhance recycling efficiency.

Recycling Method

Mechanical Recycling - Sorting and shredding plastics into flakes.
